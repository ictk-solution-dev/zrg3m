Certificate Creation Guide

Download openssl to your PC :  https://www.openssl.org

Create CA certificate

1.     CA Certificate key Generation

openssl ecparam -genkey -name prime256v1 -out ca_key_filename.key

 

2.     CA Certificate csr Generation

openssl req -new -key ca_key_filename.key -out cacert_csr_filename.csr

 

3.     CA Certificate crt Generation

openssl x509 -req -extensions v3_ca -set_serial 1 -in cacert_csr_filename.csr -signkey ca_key_filename.key  -out ca_certificate_filename.crt -extfile CA_openssl.conf

 

Create Client Certificate

1.     Client Certificate Key Generation

openssl ecparam -genkey -name prime256v1 -out client_key_filename.key

 

2.     Client Certificate csr Generation

openssl req -new -key client_key_filename.key -out clientcert_csr_filename.csr

 

3.     Client Certificate crt Generation

openssl x509 -req -extensions v3_user -in clientcert_csr_filename.csr -CA ca_certificate_filename.crt -CAcreateserial  -CAkey  ca_key_filename.key  -out client_certificate_filename.crt

 

 

*please refer the below link to use other options.
